Smoothie Recipe

Behold my famous smoothie recipe to kick start your day staight away!

Ingredients:

  • berries
  • peanut butter
  • chia seeds
  • bananas
  • dates

Steps:

  1. put the berries and bannanas in a blender
  2. put water so that the berries and bananas are covered
  3. then put in the rest of the ingredients into the blender
  4. blend the mixture for 10 seconds
  5. put smoothie into glass and enjoy
